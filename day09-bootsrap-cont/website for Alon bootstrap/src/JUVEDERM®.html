JUVÉDERM® Fillers & Lip Injections

JUVÉDERM® is a natural, injectable filler that can soften deep folds and reduce the appearance of wrinkles without resorting to surgery.

It can also be used as a filler for lip injections for full luscious lips. It is made out of hyaluronic acid which is already present in human skin and works by absorbing water and binding with collagen and elastin in the skin.

JUVÉDERM can often be a substitute for facial contouring procedures when the complaint is too minor to demand surgery. It is especially suited for the following:

  • “Smile lines” (from the nose to the corners of the mouth)
  • “Marionette lines” (below the mouth)
  • Lip augmentation
  • Hollow cheeks and other recessed areas
  • Chin contouring
Results and Aftercare for Dermal Fillers (results may vary)

Side effects include redness, pain, and tenderness, as well as some temporary swelling and bruising at the injection site. As with all injections, there is a slight risk of infection.

You should avoid exposure to the sun, heat, and cold during the first 24 hours after the treatment, as well as abstaining from alcohol and exercise. Avoid rubbing the injected area for at least 48 hours. By taking these steps, you will give the hyaluronic acid in the JUVÉDERM time to properly bind with the collagen and elastin fibres in your skin.

While your body will eventually break up the hyaluronic acid and the effects will fade after roughly 6 months to one year, you can always stop by for a touch-up treatment. Repeated treatments will mean that less JUVÉDERM will be required on each occasion.
