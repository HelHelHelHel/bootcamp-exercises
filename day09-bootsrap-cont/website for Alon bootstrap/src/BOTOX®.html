BOTOX® Cosmetic is one of the most popular cosmetic procedures in the world. It is derived from a neurotoxin produced by the bacterium Clostridium botulinum. It is applied therapeutically but it is best known for its ability to block impulses from certain facial nerves which stops the occurance of wrinkles.

BOTOX is best suited to addressing the following issues:
  • Forehead lines
  • Frown lines between the eyebrows (glabellar lines)
  • Lines beside the eyes (crow’s feet)
  • Lines around the mouth and lips (perioral wrinkles)
  • Wrinkles on the neck
  • Muscle spasms
  • Excessive sweating
  • Migraines

We will discuss the goals of your treatment first. Injecting BOTOX takes only about 15 minutes.

Results and Aftercare for BOTOX Cosmetic (results may vary)

You can return to your everyday routine immediately after the injection, although you should remain upright for 6 hours and abstain from alcohol. The effects of BOTOX are not immediate, as it takes a few days for results to appear. The effects of BOTOX are temporary, and usually last between 3-4 months, although some patients have reported longer times after repeated treatments.
